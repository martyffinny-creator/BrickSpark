What's Inside

Everything a Builder Needs

Six powerful features designed to make building more fun, educational, and social than ever before.

📸

AR Brick Scanning

Point your camera at any BrickSpark kit and watch it spring to life in augmented reality. Identify bricks instantly, see 3D overlays, and unlock hidden animations tied to your set.

📖

Step-by-Step Guides

Interactive 3D instructions you can rotate, zoom, and follow at your own pace. Never lose a paper booklet again — every guide lives in the app, forever.

🏆

Creative Challenges

Weekly themed building challenges with community voting and real prizes. Push your creativity beyond the box, then share your masterpiece with builders worldwide.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Parent Dashboard

A dedicated parent view to track building progress, manage screen time, celebrate achievements, and set healthy goals — together. Building confidence, brick by brick.

🌍

Builder Community

A safe, moderated space for young builders to share creations, leave feedback, and connect with kids who share the same passion. Creativity loves company.

🎯

Weekly Missions

Fresh missions every week come with collectible badges, XP points, and exclusive digital rewards. Complete enough missions to unlock legendary builder status.

FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

BrickSpark Studio will be available for iOS (iPhone & iPad, iOS 16+) and Android (Android 10+). The AR features require a device with ARKit or ARCore support — that covers most phones made after 2019. A full compatibility list will be published before launch.
The AR scanning and official step-by-step guides are optimised for BrickSpark kits. However, the community, challenges, and weekly missions work with any building toy. You don't need BrickSpark bricks to participate — just a love of building!
The core app — including AR scanning, step-by-step guides, and community features — is completely free to download and use. We plan to offer an optional BrickSpark Studio Pro subscription for power features like unlimited cloud saves, premium challenges, and exclusive content packs. Early access members will receive a discounted rate at launch.
Absolutely. The Builder Community is moderated 24/7 with a combination of AI content filtering and human review. Children under 13 require parental consent and are placed in age-appropriate spaces. No personal information is ever visible to other users. Parents can review, restrict, or disable social features entirely via the Parent Dashboard.
