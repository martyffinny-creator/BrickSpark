❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days within the US and Canada. Express shipping (1–2 days) is available. Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping.

Are BrickSpark products safe for young children?

Absolutely. All kits are made from non-toxic, BPA-free ABS plastic and tested to exceed ASTM F963 and EN71 safety standards.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes! 30-day no-questions-asked return policy. Contact support@brickspark.com for a free return label.

Are BrickSpark bricks compatible with LEGO?

Yes! BrickSpark bricks use the industry-standard stud size and are compatible with most major building brick brands.
